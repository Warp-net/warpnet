Warpnet is the Android client for Warpnet, a fully decentralized, peer-to-peer social network with no central servers.

Instead of signing in to a cloud account, the app pairs with your own Warpnet node — the desktop/server application you run yourself — by scanning a QR code. From then on the phone talks to your node directly over an encrypted libp2p connection.

• Truly decentralized — no company, no central server, no account to sign up for
• Pairs with your self-hosted Warpnet node via a QR code
• Direct end-to-end peer-to-peer connection (libp2p)
• Push notifications without any cloud gateway — no Firebase, no Google Cloud Messaging; a lightweight always-on connection to your node delivers them
• No Google Play Services, no proprietary dependencies — 100% free and open-source software
• Dark and light themes
• Material Design

Warpnet is free software released under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPL-3.0-or-later). The Android client is derived from Tusky.

Source code and more information: https://github.com/Warp-net/warpnet
